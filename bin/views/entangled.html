What information will others learn about me?

Starting this demo will reveal your IP address and the fact that you are running biton to other biton users, to network operators such as your Internet Service Provider, and to the BitTorrent peer discovery network.

What to expect from this demo

Your biton node is represented by the yellow circle in the center of the graph. When you connect with another user, you each generate a smaller circle with the same color in both browsers. Click on those smaller circles to swap the entangled color for both you and the other user!

Tip: Open this website in a new browser tab or in your mobile phone in order to act as multiple users. Also, see your browser console for debug messages.

Under the hood, this demo:

  • Starts a biton node within this browser tab.
  • Generates a public keypair and biton identity.
  • Joins the entangled test network, discovering other users through BitTorrent Mainline DHT.
